class Point3D extends Point

Specialization of Point to 3D case.

contains further functions to deal with corrresponding angles in space.

Example: 

  1. val p=Point(1,2,3)
val pnorm=new Point3D(p/p.norm)
val (r,theta,phi)=pnorm.toAngle()
print(r,pnorm.x)
